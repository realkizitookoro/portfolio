About Kizito Okoro

Designer. Educator. Business Coach.

My Story

With over a decade of experience in the creative industry, I've dedicated my career to helping individuals and businesses communicate their unique value through compelling visual identities, practical education, and strategic business guidance.

My journey began in graphic design, where I discovered the power of visual communication. This evolved into a passion for teaching STEM subjects, recognizing how design thinking intersects with science and technology. My work with entrepreneurs naturally developed into business coaching, helping startups and SMEs establish strong foundations for growth.

Core Competencies

  • Print & Digital Design
  • STEM Curriculum Development
  • Business Strategy Formulation
  • Startup Mentorship
  • Workshop Facilitation
